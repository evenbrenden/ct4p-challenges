2. Prove the functor laws for Reader (fmap f g = f . g).

    Preserves identity (fmap id g = id):

    fmap id g
    = id . g
    = g
    = id g

    Preserves composition (fmap (f . g) h = (fmap f) . (fmap g) $ h):

    fmap (f . g) h
    = (f . g) . h
    = f . (g . h)
    = f . (fmap g h)
    = fmap f (fmap g h)
    = (fmap f) . (fmap g) $ h
